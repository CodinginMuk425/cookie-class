Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe is from my dad and they are a favorite among friends and family. The secret ingredient is the coconut! Be warned, these will fly off of the plate!

History of cookies

Most people don't know the complicated past of the cookie. Cookies appear to have originated in the 7th century in Persia. These delicious treats shortly became popular after sugar became increasigly common in the region. By the 14th century, they were common in all Europe from royal cuisine to street vendors. More information about the history of cookies can be found here. That is how the cookie crumbles.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips
  • Oatmeal
  • Coconut

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325.
  2. Beat the butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla together until creamy.
  3. Mix together the flour, baking soda and salt in a separate bowl.
  4. Add flour mixture to butter mixture slowly.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips, oatmeal and coconut.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
